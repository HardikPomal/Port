About Me

I’m Hardik Pomal, a passionate UI/UX Designer specializing in Web Design, Mobile App Design, Web App Design, and a Graphic Design. Throughout my career, I have worn many hats, collaborating with team from various countries and across a wide range of sectors. My approach is all about blending creativity with strategy, ensuring that every design is not only visually stunning but also incredibly intuitive to use. From brainstorming ideas to fine-tuning the tiniest details, I thrive to turning concepts into design that connect with people.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working on diverse projects that brought smiles to users and satisfaction to clients. Whether it’s designing an app interface, a website, or a digital product, my goal is always the same: to create experiences that feel effortless and enjoyable. Each tap, swipe, and click should leave a lasting impression.

When I’m not immersed in design, you’ll often find me exploring the latest design trends, finding inspiration in everyday life, or relaxing with a cup of tea. Let’s work together to bring your vision to life! For a more detailed look at my qualifications and experience, feel free to connect:
