Introduction

This project focused on developing a specialized job portal website for the medical industry, connecting healthcare professionals with employers. The goal was to create an easy-to-navigate platform where users can browse job opportunities, submit applications, and connect with recruiters. The design prioritizes functionality and accessibility to support both job seekers and employers in the healthcare sector.
