Introduction

This project focused on developing a user-friendly website for the construction industry, enabling easy access to construction equipment rental services. The goal was to provide a platform that simplifies the rental process, making it easier for contractors and builders to find and rent the tools and equipment they need for their projects. The design prioritizes functionality, efficiency, and ease of use.
